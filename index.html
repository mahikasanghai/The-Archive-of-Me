Synopsis

The Archive of Me is a self-exploration journal that invites readers to reflect on who they are through the lens of powerful artworks from around the world.

By combining visual stimuli with structured psychological reflection, the journal encourages introspection on identity, emotion, memory, and personal growth—allowing adolescents and young adults to engage with their inner world in a thoughtful, accessible, and creative manner.

About the Author

Mahika Sanghai is a graduate of the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme, achieving an overall score of 37/45, including a 6 in Psychology HL and a 7 in Business Management HL.

Deeply committed to the study of psychology, Mahika is particularly interested in supporting adolescents as they explore their identities and emotional experiences. The Archive of Me reflects her academic grounding, creative sensibility, and dedication to making psychological self-reflection both meaningful and accessible.
